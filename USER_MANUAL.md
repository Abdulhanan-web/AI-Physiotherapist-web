## Overview

**AI Physio** is an intelligent physiotherapy rehabilitation platform that combines advanced AI-powered pose detection with personalized exercise programs. It helps patients recover from injuries, improve mobility, and track their rehabilitation progress through computer vision analysis and wearable device integration.

### Key Features
- **AI Pose Detection**: Real-time pose tracking using MediaPipe technology
- **Personalized Exercise Library**: 9 specialized rehabilitation exercises
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor accuracy, streaks, and performance metrics
- **Health Reports**: Generate comprehensive PDF reports of your rehabilitation journey
- **Nutrition Planning**: Personalized meal plans based on your fitness goals
- **Wearable Integration**: Connect Google Fit to sync fitness data
- **Specialty Programs**: Pre-designed rehabilitation programs for specific conditions

## Pose Detection & Exercise Tracking

### How AI Pose Detection Works

The application uses **MediaPipe Pose Detection** to analyze your movements in real-time:
- Tracks 33 body landmarks (joints and key points)
- Calculates joint angles and movement ranges
- Compares your movement to correct form patterns
- Provides accuracy scores for each repetition

### During Exercise

#### Camera Setup
1. **Distance**: Position yourself 3-5 feet from the camera
2. **Lighting**: Ensure good lighting on your body
3. **Background**: Use a clear, uncluttered background
4. **Visibility**: Keep your body and joints visible to the camera
5. **Orientation**: Face the camera directly or at the specified angle

#### Live Feedback
- **Pose Visualization**: Green skeleton overlay shows detected pose
- **Form Accuracy**: Real-time percentage of correct form
- **Rep Counter**: Automatic counting of completed repetitions
- **Angle Display**: Shows joint angles during movement

#### Accuracy Scoring
The system evaluates:
- Range of motion (ROM)
- Movement speed and control
- Symmetry and alignment
- Consistency across repetitions

**Accuracy Levels:**
- 90-100%: Excellent form
- 75-89%: Good form with minor corrections
- 60-74%: Acceptable form - focus on technique
- Below 60%: Needs improvement - review instructions

### Recording a Session

After completing an exercise:
1. Review your accuracy scores
2. Check individual rep breakdowns
3. Click "Save Session" or "Complete Exercise"
4. Provide optional notes about difficulty or pain
5. Session is recorded to your profile

### Tips for Better Accuracy
- Move slowly and controlled
- Maintain proper body alignment
- Avoid jerky or rapid movements
- Keep joints in correct range
- Maintain consistent speed throughout
- Practice regularly for improvement

## Streak Management

### Understanding Streaks

A **streak** is the number of consecutive days you've completed a specific exercise.

### How Streaks Work

1. **Starting a Streak**: Complete any exercise for the first time
2. **Maintaining**: Complete the same exercise the next day within 24 hours
3. **Growing**: Each consecutive day adds to your streak
4. **Breaking**: Miss the exercise for more than 24 hours
5. **Resetting**: Can restart a new streak anytime

### Viewing Your Streaks

1. Go to Dashboard
2. Look for "Your Streaks" section
3. See all current and previous streaks:
   - Exercise name
   - Current streak count
   - Last completion date
   - Days until streak breaks
   - Status indicators

### Streak Milestones

Achievements at key streak numbers:
- **7-Day Streak**: Bronze milestone
- **14-Day Streak**: Silver milestone
- **30-Day Streak**: Gold milestone
- **90-Day Streak**: Platinum achievement

### Streak Warnings

When you have less than 24 hours to complete an exercise:
- **Yellow Warning**: 6-12 hours remaining
- **Red Warning**: Less than 6 hours remaining
- Notification appears on dashboard
- Email reminder sent (if enabled)

### Tips for Maintaining Streaks
- Schedule exercises at the same time daily
- Complete exercises even if briefly
- Set phone reminders
- Track your progress visually
- Celebrate milestone achievements

## FAQ & Troubleshooting

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need any special equipment for exercises?**
A: No. All exercises use only your body weight and require only a clear space and camera.

**Q: How often should I exercise?**
A: This depends on your rehabilitation program. Consult your physiotherapist for personalized guidance. Daily sessions are recommended for optimal progress.

**Q: Can I use a mobile phone camera?**
A: Yes, you can use a mobile device with a webcam app, but a desktop/laptop with a stationary camera is recommended for better pose detection.

**Q: How accurate is the pose detection?**
A: The AI achieves 85-95% accuracy under ideal conditions (good lighting, clear view). Form accuracy scores help you improve technique.

**Q: What if I feel pain during exercises?**
A: Stop immediately. Pain suggests incorrect form or injury aggravation. Review instructions or consult your physiotherapist.

**Q: How long does it take to see results?**
A: Most users report noticeable improvement in 2-4 weeks with consistent daily exercise.

**Q: Can I track multiple exercises simultaneously?**
A: Yes. You can work on different exercises on different days or complete multiple exercises in one session.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely. Password reset requires email verification.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Contact support. Account deletion removes all personal data but may require confirmation period.

### Troubleshooting Guide

#### Issue: Camera Not Detected

**Problem**: Application doesn't recognize camera

**Solutions**:
1. Check camera is connected and powered on
2. Verify browser has camera permissions:
   - Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Site Settings > Camera
   - Firefox: Preferences > Privacy > Permissions > Camera
3. Close other camera-using applications
4. Refresh the page
5. Try a different browser
6. Restart your computer

#### Issue: Poor Pose Detection

**Problem**: Skeleton overlay is shaky or inaccurate

**Solutions**:
1. **Improve Lighting**: 
   - Use bright, even lighting
   - Avoid backlighting
   - Position light in front of you
2. **Increase Distance**: Position 3-5 feet away
3. **Clear Background**: Remove clutter behind you
4. **Check Camera Quality**: Use webcam with at least 720p resolution
5. **Keep Body Visible**: Full body should be in frame
6. **Stable Position**: Stand on flat, stable ground

#### Issue: Low Accuracy Scores

**Problem**: Consistently getting below 70% accuracy

**Solutions**:
1. **Slow Down Movements**: Move more deliberately
2. **Review Instructions**: Watch step-by-step guide carefully
3. **Check Form**: Look at demonstration videos
4. **Optimize Setup**: Ensure good lighting and camera angle
5. **Isolate Movements**: Move only the target joints
6. **Practice**: Accuracy improves with repetition
7. **Video Yourself**: Record yourself and compare to demonstrations

#### Issue: Cannot Log In

**Problem**: Login credentials not accepted

**Solutions**:
1. Verify email address is correct
2. Check caps lock isn't on
3. Use "Forgot Password" if unsure
4. Ensure account was verified (check email)
5. Clear browser cookies:
   - Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Delete browsing data
   - Firefox: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Data
6. Try different browser
7. Try incognito/private mode

#### Issue: Report Generation Fails

**Problem**: Cannot generate health reports

**Solutions**:
1. Ensure you have completed at least 1 exercise
2. Check internet connection is stable
3. Refresh the page
4. Try different report type
5. Clear browser cache
6. Check available storage space
7. Try downloading to different location

#### Issue: Google Fit Connection Failed

**Problem**: Cannot connect wearable device

**Solutions**:
1. Verify Google account credentials
2. Ensure wearable device has data synced
3. Check Google Fit app is installed and active
4. Verify wearable device is paired to phone
5. Try disconnecting and reconnecting
6. Clear browser cookies and try again
7. Ensure Google account has no 2FA blocking the connection

#### Issue: Slow Application Performance

**Problem**: App is lagging or slow

**Solutions**:
1. Close other browser tabs and applications
2. Restart your browser
3. Clear browser cache:
   - Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Delete browsing data
   - Firefox: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Data
4. Check internet speed (minimum 5 Mbps recommended)
5. Disable browser extensions (they may interfere)
6. Try a different browser
7. Update your browser to latest version

#### Issue: Streak Broke Unexpectedly

**Problem**: Streak counter reset when you completed exercise

**Solutions**:
1. Verify exercise was completed within 24 hours
2. Check that completion was recorded (refresh page)
3. Contact support if issue persists
4. Note: Streaks reset if more than 24 hours pass since last completion
